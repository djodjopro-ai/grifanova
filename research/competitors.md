# Competitor Analysis — Serbian Custom Interior Design Firms

*Focus: Small/medium custom interior studios comparable to Grifa*

---

## 1. Kraken Design
- **URL:** https://krakendesign.rs
- **Type:** Custom interior design studio + furniture manufacturing
- **Location:** Belgrade
- **Positioning:** Premium, modern — "Dizajn enterijera za one koji ne pristaju na prosečno"

### Website Structure:
1. **Hero** — Full-width image with headline "Projektovanje i dizajn enterijera" + subheadline + CTA "Kontaktiraj nas"
2. **Value proposition** — "MODERNO. JEDINSTVENO. TVOJE." with compelling copy
3. **Services (3 cards):**
   - Dizajn Stambenih Enterijera
   - Dizajn Poslovnih Enterijera
   - Dizajn i Izrada Nameštaja po Meri
4. **Benefits section** — 3D visualization, virtual tour, precision manufacturing
5. **Gallery teaser** — "Naša Vizija Dizajna" with CTA to portfolio page
6. **CTA banner** — "Usudi se da sanjaš veliko" + "Zakaži Konsultaciju"
7. **Testimonials** — 4 client reviews with names
8. **Contact** — Phone, email, working hours + contact form (Ime, Email, Telefon, Poruka)
9. **FAQ** — 8 collapsible questions

### Key Patterns:
- WebP images served via CDN (CloudFront) — performance-focused
- Strong copywriting in Serbian — conversational "ti" form (informal)
- Testimonials with real names build trust
- FAQ section reduces friction
- "Besplatna konsultacija" (free consultation) as primary CTA
- Contact form: Ime, Email, Telefon, Poruka (same fields we planned)
- Clean modern design, no clutter

### Strengths: Best design of all competitors, strong copy, trust signals
### Weaknesses: No visible portfolio on homepage (links to separate page)

## 2. Design Studio In
- **URL:** https://design-studioin.com
- **Type:** Architectural interior design studio
- **Location:** Novi Sad & Belgrade
- **Positioning:** Luxury — "Luxurious space & Lifestyle"

### Website Structure:
1. **Hero** — Logo + "Luxurious space & Lifestyle" headline
2. **Founder section** — Personal brand (Marijana Jovin, Master Inženjer Arhitekture) with photo + story
3. **Stats bar** — Years of experience, completed projects, years independent work
4. **Services (3 numbered):**
   - Projektovanje (Design)
   - Vizualizacija (3D renders/animation)
   - Izvođenje radova (Construction management)
5. **YouTube section** — Embedded video presentations of completed projects
6. **Testimonials** — 4 client reviews with photos and cities
7. **Internship offer** — "Budi deo našeg tima"
8. **Business interiors section**
9. **Social media links** — Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn, Pinterest
10. **Multi-language** — EN, DE, RU, SR toggle

### Key Patterns:
- **Personal brand** centered on architect founder — builds trust via expertise
- YouTube video tours of completed projects — rich content
- Stats/counter animations (years, projects)
- Testimonials from multiple cities (Belgrade, Frankfurt, Novi Sad)
- Multi-language support for international clients
- Elementor/WordPress site

### Strengths: Personal touch, video content, international reach, testimonials
### Weaknesses: Somewhat cluttered, duplicate content sections, WordPress template feel

### Key Patterns:
- Heavy SEO/content marketing approach — long articles on homepage
- Targets diaspora clients (Serbs abroad buying apartments)
- Emphasizes "ključ u ruke" (turnkey) service
- Phone CTA prominent (064/5098522)
- 3D model/render as key selling point
- Budget-friendly positioning
- Blog-style content mixed with portfolio

### Strengths: Great SEO content, diaspora targeting, detailed process explanation
### Weaknesses: Too text-heavy, outdated design, no clear visual hierarchy

## Strategic Insights for Grifa

### What the best competitors do:
| Pattern | Who does it | Should Grifa? |
|---------|------------|---------------|
| Hero video/rich media | Design Studio In (YouTube) | **YES — but AI-generated cinematic video, unique in market** |
| "Besplatna konsultacija" CTA | Kraken, Moja 4 Zida | **YES — primary CTA** |
| Client testimonials | Kraken, Design Studio In | **YES — add later with real reviews** |
| Process explanation | Moja 4 Zida (detailed) | **YES — visual timeline** |
| Portfolio filtering | Stolar | **YES — with superior design** |
| Stats/counters | Design Studio In | **YES — years, projects** |
| Contact form (Ime, Email, Tel, Poruka) | Kraken | **YES — same fields** |
| FAQ section | Kraken | **Consider for later** |
| Personal founder story | Design Studio In | **Ask client** |

### Where Grifa will differentiate:
1. **AI-generated hero video** — No competitor has this. Cinematic, not a YouTube embed
2. **Portfolio quality** — Grifa's real photos show very high-end modern work
3. **Visual design** — Most competitors use WordPress templates. Our custom Tailwind build will be cleaner
4. **Category depth** — 5 categories with filtering (Kuhinje, Kupatila, Plakari, Komode, Dnevni boravak)
5. **Process section** — Visual timeline, not a text wall like Moja 4 Zida

### CTA language (Serbian market standard):
- "Zatražite besplatnu konsultaciju" / "Zakaži konsultaciju" (Request/Schedule free consultation)
- "Kontaktiraj nas" (Contact us)
- "Pogledaj galeriju radova" (See portfolio)
- "Pošalji poruku" (Send message)

### Recommended tone:
- Use informal "ti" form like Kraken Design (modern, approachable) rather than formal "Vi"
- Short, punchy copy — not walls of text like Moja 4 Zida
- Confident positioning: custom, high-quality, modern
